# Burp Suite Sozlash

1. **Proxy** tabiga o'ting.
2. **Intercept is on** tugmasini bosing.
3. Brauzerda FoxyProxy orqali `127.0.0.1:8080` manzilini kiriting.

Endi barcha trafik Burp orqali o'tadi.
